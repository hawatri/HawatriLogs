How to Add Images to Your Blog

Images can make your blog posts more engaging and help illustrate your points. In Hawatri, we've created a simple way to add images with captions to your blog posts.

Basic Image with Caption

Here's how you can add a simple image with a caption to your blog:

<div class="blog-image">
  <img src="blog/images/your-image.jpg" alt="Description of the image">
  <div class="blog-image-caption">Your caption text here</div>
</div>

Tips for Using Images

  • Always provide meaningful alt text for accessibility
  • Use high-quality images that are properly sized
  • Keep captions concise and informative
  • Consider the layout and flow of your content
  • Optimize images for web performance
